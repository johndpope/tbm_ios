lazi 
  - idea: I am too lazy to do anything but press and release this button.
  - verb: lazi me when you get home. 
  - noun: send me a lazi
  - Web and app names available.
  - Problem with misspelling is that people search for the correct spelling. But early on it can be the first search result since there is little competition. Also misspelling can be trademarked.

lazy
  - same as Lazi
  - Lazyapp.com available on web for $2000
  - Problem with real spelling is it brings up tons of more popular hits in the store. But if it gets popular it might eventually become the first search result. Difficult to trademark.
  
favi
  - idea: just my favorites
  - verb: favi me. noun: send me a favi
  - looks available on iphone and little competition on android. faviapp.com available.
  
favo
  - Same idea as favi with similar availability and competition. favoapp.com taken.
